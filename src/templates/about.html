Our Vision

SwelCoin was born from curiosity and a desire to understand how blockchain works at its core. It's not just a cryptocurrency — it's a sandbox for learning, experimenting, and innovating.

How It Works

  • 🧱 Blocks are created with each new transaction batch.
  • 🔐 The server signature validates new blocks and adds security.
  • 🌐 Anyone can contribute, review, and explore the chain transparently.
